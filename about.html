ML Insights Engine

Your website learns. So does your strategy.

We embed a proprietary machine learning reporting system into every project. It tracks how real users behave, surfaces patterns automatically and delivers clear, actionable recommendations — no data team required on your end.

📊
Behavioural Tracking

Monitors what users click, scroll past, hover on and ignore — building a real-time picture of how people actually move through your site.

🧠
Sentiment Analysis

Detects frustration signals like rage clicks and rapid exits, alongside delight patterns like long dwell times and return visits, to reveal the emotional quality of each session.

🎯
Product Reaction Mapping

Identifies which services or products attract attention vs. cause drop-off — so you know exactly where to invest and what to reconsider.

📈
Automated Weekly Reports

Plain-English summaries delivered to your client dashboard every week — key patterns highlighted with a short list of recommended actions, no jargon.

🔍
Cohort Comparison

Compares how different audience segments respond to the same content — enabling you to tailor messaging and layout for the people who matter most to your business.

💬
Conversion Intelligence

Tracks the micro-moments that lead to conversions and those that don't — making it possible to test, learn and improve on a continuous loop without guesswork.

📣 Clients using our ML reports see an average 40% improvement in conversion rate within the first 90 days post-launch.

AI Project Consultant

Meet Craft — your AI project consultant.

Every TechCraft client gets access to Craft, an AI chatbot built into their client portal. Craft bridges the gap between what you're imagining and what we need to build — no technical vocabulary required.

Understands plain language

Describe your vision however feels natural — "clean and modern but bold" — and Craft asks the right follow-up questions to pin down exactly what you mean, then maps it to a service package.

Generates your project brief

Based on your conversation, Craft produces a structured brief covering audience, goals, timeline and budget range — and sends it directly to the TechCraft team, ready for scoping.

Reads your ML reports for you

After launch, Craft interprets your weekly insights and surfaces clear, plain-English recommendations: "Your hero section has a 72% scroll-past rate — consider shortening the copy."

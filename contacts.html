Campaigns can move faster — often 2–4 weeks — because the creative direction is narrower. After our discovery call, we provide a detailed timeline as part of the scoping document so you always know what to expect.

Yes — the majority of our work is delivered remotely, so location is not a barrier. We've collaborated with clients across Africa, Europe and North America. All reviews, communication and handoffs happen digitally, and we accommodate different time zones for calls and presentations.

You don't need a polished brief. A rough sense of your goals, your audience and what success looks like is enough. Our AI consultant Craft will ask follow-up questions and generate a structured brief automatically. If you prefer, book a discovery call and we'll extract everything we need in conversation.

Once your site launches, we embed lightweight behavioural tracking that captures how users interact with every element — scroll depth, click patterns, dwell time and exit signals. Our ML models process this data weekly and deliver a plain-English report to your dashboard, highlighting what's working, what isn't, and what to prioritise next. No data science background required to act on it.

Craft is an AI chatbot built into the TechCraft client portal. It helps you describe what you want in plain language, asks guided questions about audience, goals, budget and timeline, then recommends the right service package. After launch, it reads your weekly ML reports and surfaces specific, plain-English recommendations — so you always know what to improve next without having to interpret raw data yourself.

Absolutely. Our Portfolio page showcases selected case studies with context on the brief, approach and outcome. For work relevant to a specific industry or problem, email us and we'll share a curated set of case studies directly. We can also walk through them during a discovery call if that's more useful.

Standard packages include two structured revision rounds — typically after initial concepts and again after refined designs. These are consolidated rounds where all feedback is gathered in one go, which keeps projects moving efficiently. Additional revisions beyond that scope are priced separately.

Yes. We offer monthly retainer arrangements that include continued ML reporting, design and content updates, Craft access and priority support. Retainer clients also get early access to new TechCraft features and quarterly strategy reviews. Retainer scope and pricing is discussed during the handover session at the close of your initial project.
